Course content


1. Understanding the Passive Voice



2. Students: 6th grade



3. Lesson time: 80 minutes



4. Objective: To identify and recognize the use of the Passive voice with the correct structure and understanding the context.



5. Subject: Passive Voice



6. First activity: Every student must think on three things they have done for someone else (helping to do something, to cook, to clean, etc). This way the students will use the past tense beside the vocabulary studied in class, in order to have the structure to formulate the passive voice later. (time 5 minutes)



7. Development activity: In this section the student will share to the class, one of the things he did for someone else, and using those sentences the students will stablish the way and form to structure the passive voice. Using the reference of learning Zone book on page 68, the teacher will explain the structure and the correct use of Passive Voice, then the students will listen the conversation and underline the sentences with passive voice.



8. Closing activity: organized in pairs, the students will share to another the three things they wrote at the beginning using the correct structure of passive voice and helping each other if there is a mistake on any sentence.
